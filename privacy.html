Privacy Policy

General Website Privacy Notice

At DataPioneers, we are committed to protecting your privacy and maintaining the highest standards of data protection.

Effective Date: July 7, 2017 | Last Revised: October 23, 2023

Description of DataPioneers' Business

DataPioneers LLC is a global technology company that maintains an online insights exchange platform connecting community owners to researchers, agencies, and brands for sharing and accessing consumer data.

We Protect Your Privacy

DataPioneers is committed to protecting the privacy and security of all Personal Data collected or received. We strive to conform our privacy practices with applicable international, national, federal, state, and local laws and regulations.

Why We Collect Data

  • To keep you informed about our products, services, and activities
  • Statutory and regulatory compliance
  • To process your inquiries and communications
  • To market our products and services
  • To send you surveys about our products and services

How We Collect Information

We collect information through:

  • Our website
  • Business meetings
  • Promotions and events
  • Telephone calls and email communications

Information Security

We maintain appropriate technical, administrative, and physical safeguards to protect your information. However, no transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

Your Rights

You can access, review, correct, or delete your information by contacting our Privacy Compliance Officer.
